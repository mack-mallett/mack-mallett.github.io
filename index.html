Data Analysis: Wet and Dry Years

This Notebook analyzes the relationship between monthly rainfall and wildfire intensity in the Southeast Fire Center of British Columbia. I use python scripts with the pandas package to clean and transform data sourced from WildFire BC and Environment Canada. Using statistics to model the relationship sheds light on how rainfall in the 1-6 months prior determines the destruction caused by wildfires in my home province.
